I'm Cory Paisley, a recent computer science graduate from Westchester Community College. I'd also consider myself a writer and aspiring game designer. I have a deep passion for both the reading and writing of provocative stories, and even more than that I love seeing them come to life- through motion and animation.

To me, a great piece of art simply leaves what can only be interpreted as a feeling. I don't even know if I can properly describe it or do the feeling justice. It can vary from person to person.It could be that tenderness a viewer can feel in their legs; it can be an under-the-breath, inaudible "Wow...". To me, it's the feeling of actually living, it's a young, immature feeling; rebellious even. That dangerous feeling of thinking, just for a moment, that anything could be possible. I want to be able to-If I may- deliver that amazing feeling to an audience of my own some day. Computer Science is the road I've chosen to take toward fulfilling that vision.
